Our Story

Inspired by the warm and inviting atmosphere of a coffee shop, this website was designed to create a comfortable and enjoyable experience for every visitor. It reflects the calm, creativity, and connection that coffee shops bring to people.


This website was created by Group 4 of 11-ICT 4, led by Johnnero Monfero. It is a collaborative project for Computer Programming 3 and 4. This website would not have been possible without our beloved friends, whose guidance and support helped make this coffee shop–inspired website a reality.
